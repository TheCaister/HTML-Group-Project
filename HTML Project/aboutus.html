WHAT DO WE DO?

GIVEAWAYS

Every month, we give away free games to people that really need it. We also give away consoles and even PCs so that your day will be that much brighter! Whether it's for coping, forgetting the outside world for a few seconds or just alleviating boredom, we believe everyone should have the right to enjoy video games!

HOW DO WE PAY FOR EVERYTHING?

CHARITY STREAMS

We host a weekly livestream where we play the latest games, as well as our monthly challenge livestream where game related challenges are chosen by the audience and chaos ensues! Our main donation driver is the annual speed-run event where talented gamers finish their favourite games in world record time during the midst of a 24hr livestream.

SPONSORS

Soon after our inception, companies from all around the world have had their hearts touched by our mission. As a result, they are able to sponsor our efforts and even donate copies of their latest games!
