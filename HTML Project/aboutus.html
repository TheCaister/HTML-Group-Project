HOW DO WE PAY FOR EVERYTHING?

CHARITY STREAMS

We host a weekly livestream where we play the latest games, as well as our monthly challenge livestream where game related challenges are chosen by the audience and chaos ensues. Our main donation driver, is the annual speed-run event where talented gamers finish their favourite games in world record time during the midst of a 24hr livestream.

GIVEAWAYS

Every month, we give away some free games. We also give away consoles and even PCs so that your day will be that much brighter!
