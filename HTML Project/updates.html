CORK UNIVERSITY HOSPITAL

Earlier this month, we donated three PS4’s and a Nintendo Switch to Cork University Hospital. The children were delighted about this gift and it was all thanks to you, our generous donators! Here is a photo of one of the children who now have a fantastic way to spend their time.

CHARITY CRATE

We are very excited to announce that come the Summer of 2021, in partnership with various Indie developers, the Charity Crate will be released! Charity Crate is a selection of Indie games that vary monthly and the price is based on the amount you want to pay! Charity Crate is a game subscription that lets you play great games and contribute to the joy of all!
